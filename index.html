About Me

Hi! My name is Georgiy Kiselev and I am a Undergraduate Statistical Machine Learning student at the University of California - Davis. I am very passionate about raising quality of life through ML/AI applications.

I love reading about new innovations in the realm of Deep Learning, and hope to make my own relevant contributions in the future.

I have previously collaborated with several different Machine Learning / Data Science organizations at UC Davis to lead the development of unique ML/AI projects aimed at making a positive impact.
If you're interested in hearing more about them, don't hesitate to contact me via any of the methods in the 'Contact' section of the website!

I am enthusiastic to continue learning and growing as an ML engineer and data scientist and am open to new opportunities to utilize my skills and make a positive change in the world.
